Conservation and Ecological
Research Guide


This guide is intended to facilitate the information discovery activities of researchers working on ecological conservation and forest management issues and topics. It provide links to tools, materials, and resources on ecological conservation and related disciplines. This guide was born from frustration I encountered when working on my graduate thesis and had difficulty finding the right materials and resources to reference in my work. Even though the information and resources provided here are not exhaustive, every effort has been made to offer as comprehensive a list as possible. Also, this resource must be viewed as a living, dynamic document, meaning that it will be updated frequentlty as and when new information and data becomes available. Last updated: March 19, 2021
