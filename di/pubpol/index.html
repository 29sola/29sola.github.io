LIBR 533: Legal Information Sources & Services

  • Click here to view the copy I submitted for grading
  • This work was a final assignment for the course, Legal Information Sources and Services, and it tested the knowledge and competencies that we - course members - had developed on the conduct of legal research. It examined the skills we'd acquired from the course in discovering legal documents and presenting them to various audiences. This course helped me develop competencies in search techniques for retrieving legal and legislative information from both federal, provincial, academic and international databases (LegisInfo, LexisNexis, BC Laws, BC Legislative Assembly database, Parliament of Canada database, CanLii etc.
